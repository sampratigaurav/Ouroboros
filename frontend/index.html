How to Play

  • SPACE — Dash (burst of speed)

Dash Ability

Activate dash to temporarily double your speed. Costs one tail segment and has a 5-second cooldown. Use it to escape danger or close in on prey.

Trap Ability

Place a trap behind your snake. Any enemy that hits it dies instantly. Traps are temporary and have a cooldown. Use them strategically to control the arena.

Dynamic Arena

The arena slowly shrinks over time, pushing all players into a tighter space. Stay inside the boundary or die.

Wormholes

Portals appear on the map. Enter one wormhole and teleport to its partner. Use them for surprise attacks or quick escapes.

Victory

The last snake alive wins. Eat food to grow, avoid walls, yourself, and other snakes. Eliminate opponents to climb the leaderboard.

About Ouroboros

Ouroboros is a modern real-time multiplayer competitive Snake arena. The classic game reimagined with special abilities, a dynamic shrinking arena, wormhole teleportation, and server-authoritative networking.

Features

  • Real-time multiplayer (2-5 players)
  • Server-authoritative game engine
  • Dash & Trap abilities
  • Shrinking arena mechanic
  • Wormhole teleportation
  • Global leaderboard

Tech Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Canvas API
  • Backend: Node.js, Express
  • Networking: Socket.IO (WebSockets)
